ナカイ スコット (Scott Nakai) is a multimedia artist, specializing in both 3D and 2D art, music composition and production, and game development. They started working in 2018, and have not been stopped yet. Currently, the big project they're working on is モリのコンチェルト, stylized in English as FOREST CONCERTO. It is a game about an ancient Forest being invaded by an army of robots who wish to pillage it for its resources. You take control of a bamboo person, named TAKEDA, whose job it is as the Will of the Forest to help bring the varying groups and interests among those who inhabit the Forest to bring peace back, once and for all. The demo is set to release in late Summer of 2022.
